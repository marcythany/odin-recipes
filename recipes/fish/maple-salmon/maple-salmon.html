Maple Salmon

This maple glazed salmon is delicious and very easy to prepare. I love maple syrup in everything and decided to use it in the marinade. My husband totally loved it; he wasn't a salmon fan until now.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup fish sauce
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 Salmon

Steps

  1. Combine the ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Add the salmon to the bowl and mix well.
  3. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Serve with rice.
